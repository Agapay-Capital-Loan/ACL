ABOUT US


OUR JOURNEY

Agapay Capital Loan started on May 9, 2015, in the historic streets of Intramuros, Manila. Built on the idea of “agapay,” meaning support, we set out to offer more than just loans. We wanted to be a helping hand for individuals and small businesses trying to get ahead. The name also carries the initials of our founders Afable, Cruz, and Licudo, a reminder that this company was born out of shared dreams, hard work, and a deep desire to make a difference.

We began at a time when the Philippines was growing fast, and so were the hopes of many Filipinos looking to build something of their own. Over the past 10 years, we’ve stayed true to our values: keep things simple, stay open, and treat every customer with care. As we continue to grow, our mission remains the same. We walk alongside our clients, offering guidance, support, and the financial tools they need to thrive.

GENERAL FAQs

What is Agapay Capital Loan (ACL)?
Agapay Capital Loan is a customer-focused financial service provider offering online microloans, financial education, and personalized insights to help individuals manage and improve their financial well-being.
Who can apply for a loan at Agapay Capital Loan (ACL)?
ACL is open to all hardworking Filipinos, particularly working individuals and micro or small business owners who may be underserved by traditional banks.
Why does ACL focus on working individuals and small business owners?
Because we believe financial support should be a lifeline, not a privilege. These groups often face barriers with banks, so ACL provides fast, accessible, and flexible loan solutions designed for their real-world needs.
Do I need to have a business to apply for a loan?
No. While we serve small business owners, any employed individual with a valid ID, proof of income, and a financial account is welcome to apply.
What makes ACL different from traditional banks?
Unlike many banks, ACL emphasizes speed, simplicity, and customer care, making our services easier to access and more responsive to everyday financial challenges.
I’m self-employed or a freelancer—can I still apply?
Yes! Whether you're self-employed, a freelancer, or running a small shop, ACL is here to support your financial journey with personalized and flexible options.

For Working Individuals (Low to Middle Income Earners)

Can I apply for a loan even if I don’t have a high-paying job?
Yes. ACL is built to serve hardworking Filipinos, especially low to middle-income earners like office workers, freelancers, delivery riders, and household helpers.
What if I don’t have formal employment?
That’s okay! We also support self-employed and informal workers who can show stable income or activity, even without standard employment records.
What kind of expenses can ACL loans help with?
ACL loans can help with bills (electricity, rent, water), tuition, medical needs, emergencies, or even bridging payday gaps.
How do I apply for a loan?
You can apply online using our app or website, or reach out to one of our local agents for assistance. The process is fast, simple, and beginner-friendly.
Are there hidden charges?
No. We offer a transparent process with no hidden fees, and we explain everything clearly, so you always know what you’re signing up for.

For Micro or Small Business Owners

I own a sari-sari store. Can I get a loan from ACL?
Yes! ACL provides specialized microloans for small businesses like sari-sari stores, food stalls, online sellers, and home-based enterprises.
What are the requirements to qualify?
Your business must be operating for at least 6 months to 1 year, showing basic stability and income. We require minimal paperwork.
What can I use the loan for?
You can use the loan to restock inventory, cover slow seasons, buy equipment, or manage cash flow during income dips.
I don’t have formal business documents or collateral. Can I still apply?
Yes. We understand that many small business owners operate informally. We assess your dedication, consistency, and earning activity, not just paperwork.
Will someone help me if I’m unsure how to use the loan wisely?
Definitely. ACL offers personalized support and insights to help you make informed decisions, tailored to your business goals.

QUESTIONS FOR OUR SERVICES

Online Microloans FAQs

What is an Online Microloan?
An Online Microloan is a fast, simple, and flexible short-term loan offered through ACL’s digital platform to help you address urgent financial needs.
How much can I borrow?
You can borrow any amount from ₱5,000 up to ₱125,000, depending on your eligibility.
How do I apply for a loan?
Applications are done entirely online through a user-friendly app. No long paperwork—just a few easy steps!
How long is the repayment period?
You can choose to repay your loan in 6 to 24 months, based on the loan amount and your eligibility.
What is the interest rate?
The average monthly interest rate is 1.89%.
How will I receive my loan?
Once approved, your loan amount will be transferred to your preferred financial account, and you will receive a confirmation via email.

Financial Literacy Hub FAQs

What is the Financial Literacy Hub?

Who can access the Financial Literacy Hub?
All ACL clients and loan applicants are welcome to use the hub for free.
What can I learn from the Hub?
Topics include budgeting, saving, debt management, and other practical money habits tailored to everyday life.
How are the materials delivered?
You can access step-by-step guides and weekly tips online through your account or email.

Personalized Financial Insights FAQs

What are Personalized Financial Insights?

How does it work?
Insights and tips are delivered through email, based on analysis of your financial habits, loan behavior, and budgeting needs.
What kind of tips will I receive?
You’ll receive automated reminders, personalized budgeting suggestions, and strategic tips to avoid late fees and build stronger financial habits.
Is this service free?
Yes, it's a free value-added service available to all ACL clients.
